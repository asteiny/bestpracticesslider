How to use this

  1. Pick the place type that best fits the area you're thinking about.
  2. If you like, choose your jurisdiction to see its scores today — the tool will suggest which Ds have the most room to improve.
  3. Move through the five Ds, turning on actions and setting the sliders to match — or test — local policy.
  4. Each category earns Bronze, Silver, or Gold, and your overall tier updates live in the scoreboard.
  5. Tap any i for a plain-language explanation of what an action means.
